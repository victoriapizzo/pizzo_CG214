How much does installation cost?

Installation is free with your purchase of one of our fingerprint scanners. (Tips are appreciated though!)

Can Mood Lighting be installed in my pre-existing light fixtures?

Yes, we can hook our scanners up to your pre-existing light fixtures as long as you use one of our color-temperature changing bulbs.

How will Mood Lighting help me?

Your brain reacts to the color temperature of light. Our technology finds the best color temperature to keep your mood balanced. This can help fight depression or just help you unwind after a long day. Find more information in our About page!

Can my room be a different color temperature than another room in the house?

Yes. The system can synchronize each room in your house that is hooked up, or they can be separated so that different rooms can have different color temperatures based on the person who is occupying it.

How many scanners will I need in my home?

A scanner will be needed for every room that you want Mood Lighting to be in.
