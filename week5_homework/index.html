Solace: An Interactive Animated Film

Description

Solace is an interactive video narrating the story of a boy who becomes obsessed with the endless flavor combination possibilites of a soda. The website allows the viewer to interact with the animations as a voice narrates the story. Using the mouse, the viewer can pop bubbles and shake the figures on the screen as the story progresses.

The Website

On the about page, the viewer can click a link that leads to descriptions and videos describing the team's entire process, from coming up with the idea to implementing the effects in the video. There is also a link to download a "press pack" which comes with:

  • gifs
  • screenshots
  • pictures of the crew
  • a movie poster
  • an overview PDF about the video, its nominations, and its creators.

The other websites and projects that I looked at did not keep my attention, and I soon found them boring. Solace was the only one that captured my attention for enough time to really enjoy it. I love how colorful and lighthearted it is, while simultaneously teaching an important lesson. It is fun and interactive while keeping an interesting narrative.
